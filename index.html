Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data in our AI-powered chatbot application.

Last Updated: August 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nexora AI, your intelligent virtual assistant developed by Tahmid. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Chat messages: Processed in real-time to generate responses

2.2 Information We Don't Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or precise location
  • Persistent chat logs (conversations are temporary)

3. Why We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our chatbot services
  • To personalize your experience (theme preferences)
  • To maintain app functionality and security

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

All user data is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • Android's Room Database for chat history
  • SharedPreferences for app settings

You can clear this data anytime through clear data or by uninstalling the app.

4.2 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all API communications
  • Regular security audits of our codebase

5. Third-Party Services

Our chatbot uses these trusted providers:

  • Groq API: For AI processing (view their Privacy)
  • Firebase: For anonymous crash reporting (disabled by default)

We minimize data shared with third parties and anonymize requests where possible.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: View all stored data via device settings
  • Delete: Clear app data or uninstall
  • Opt-out: Disable permissions in device settings

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices.

  • Updates on our website

8. Contact Us

For questions about this policy or data requests:

  • Email: tahmidbusinessyt@gmail.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours
